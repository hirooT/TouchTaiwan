五軸加工機主軸動態負載感測方案
WThe spindle dynamic load sensing solution for 5-axis machining machine
高強度薄型力感測器可內嵌於工具機主軸中，量測主軸受力特 性，搭配邊緣運算模組即時診斷軸承異常狀態並給予警示信 息，使工具機設備達到預期性維護作業排定，便可降低非預期 性停機風險。
The high-strength and thin force sensor can be embedded in the machine tool spindle to measure the force characteristics of the spindle. In addition, with the edge computing module, it can immediately determine the damage status of the spindle and provide warning information. Let machine tools reduce the risk of unplanned downtime and scheduled maintenance work.
